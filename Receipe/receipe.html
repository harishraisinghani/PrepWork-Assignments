Palak Paneer Receipe - A Classic Indian Dish!

By: Harish Raisinghani

Palak paneer is one of the most popular indian dishes, consisting of soft paneer (or cottage cheese cubes) cooked in a smooth spinach curry.

Palak paneer goes well with rice and naan. It can also be easily made vegan friendly by substituting the paneer with tofu


Ingredients

The following ingredients will provide a serving for 4.

Quantity Ingredient
3oz Bags of baby spinach
1 Large onion
1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon Ground cardamom
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/3 teaspoon Chopped garlic
1/3 cup Chopped tomato
2 tablespoons Plain yogurt
1 tablespoon Coriander powder
1/3 teaspoon Garam masala
1/3 teaspoon Paprika
1/3 teaspoon Salt
5oz Paneer cheese, in cubes
1/4 cup Heavy cream

Directions

  • Cut spinach into shreds and cook in 3 tablespoons of water until tender; remove from heat.
  • Saute onion, cinnamon, cardamom, and ginger in 1-2 tablespoons of oil until onions are translucent. Then add garlic and chopped tomatoes, and reduce heat.
  • Cook this mix briefly and slowly blend in a little bit of yogurt to prevent curdling.
  • Add coriander, garam masala, paprika, and salt, and mix well.
  • Add cooked spinach with liquid, cover and simmer on low heat for approximately 20-30 minutes.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Take half of the spinach mixture and puree in food processor or blender, and return to mixture and stir.
  • Slowly stir in heavy cream on low heat.
  • Add in the paneer cubes (or tofu).
  • Serve with rice or naan!
